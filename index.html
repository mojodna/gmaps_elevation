Gmaps Elevation

Elevation displayed on Google Maps using a heatmap layer

This map shows the highest points in San Francisco as dark red, the lowest points with no color, and in between as green or yellow. All the coloration is relative to what's currently in view, so if you zoom in, you can see the contours of a neighborhood in as much detail as you can see the whole city.
